About Us

IT varsity focusses on 3 levels of learning to make our students highly successful: 1 - Accredited qualifications 2 - Practical, marketable skills 3 - Entrepreneurship skills

Vision: IT varsity aims to transform the technology sector in South Africa by producing a new generation of tech innovators and entrepreneurs.

Mission: to provide targeted training and learning and create an environment that leverages South Africa’s National Development plan to promote job creation and skills development.

Our philosophy For Training: At IT Varsity we firmly believe in the age-old principle that if you want to become an expert, you need to be trained by one.
That’s what makes IT Varsity different!
